About Us

R.R.BUILDERS

R.R.Builders is a second generation company that was established as Radhakrishna Constructions by late Mr.T.A.Radhakrishnan in 1972. Since its inception, the company has focused on changing the face of real-estate in Chennai with innovative and quality construction projects. In the past 35 years, the company has built more than 300 Projects spread across 15,00,000 square feet of land in Chennai.

The business was successfully taken over by Mr.K.R.Vijayaraghavan in the year 2008 under the name of R.R.Builders.

With a clear vision to construct Apartments, Buildings, Bungalows, Factories and Commercial Establishments, the company has emerged as one of the leading and most reliable names in the field of construction, Real-estate and property development.

The company is a perfect blend of the experience and sagacity of an older generation, and the dynamism and motivation of a younger one. Its strong foundations are represented by its team of dedicated and competent engineers, proficient supervisors and skilled workers, thanks to whom the company has earned a reputation for timely completion.

Having cemented relationships over 300 projects with over 1.5 million sq.ft. of built up area and over 500 satisfied customers, the company is proud to say that it still treats each new project with equal importance and enthusiasm.
